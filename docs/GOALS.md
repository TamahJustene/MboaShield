## Score targets (self-audit before pitch)

Rate yourself 1–10 on each. **Below 8 = fix before bootcamp.**

1. Pertinence (theme 2026)  
2. Innovation (not a clone of foreign tools)  
3. Technical feasibility (demo always works)  
4. Socio-economic impact (who benefits in Cameroon?)  
5. Business model (freemium + B2B clear)  
6. AI justified (each AI feature has a reason)  
7. Digital patriotism & cybersecurity (core, not decoration)  
8. Pitch clarity (3 min, no jargon)  
9. Backup plan (offline video if Wi-Fi dies)  
10. Dossier honesty (prototype stage, not fake scale)

**Minimum to win:** average **8.5+** on items 1, 3, 7, 8.
